About Us

Agni Dance Club are a local community group and they focus on developing local talent through vocational courses for every age group. We provides outstanding technical training in a positive environment that focuses on the life skills of dependability, punctuality, responsibility, respect, and teamwork. We offer seven(7) different types of dance classes in a variety of styles and levels, so there is a class for everyone!

Our Classes

Tap Dance

Tap dance is a type of dance characterised by using the sounds of tap shoes striking the floor as a form of percussion. The sound is made by shoes that have a metal "tap" on the heel and toe.

Jazz Dance

Jazz dance is a performance dance technique and style that first emerged in the United States in the early twentieth century. Jazz dance may refer to vernacular jazz or to Broadway or theatrical jazz.

Lyrical Dance

Lyrical dance has been defined as a dance style that embodies various aspects of ballet, jazz, acrobatics & Modern dance while still maintaining its autonomy. So You Think You Can Dance.
