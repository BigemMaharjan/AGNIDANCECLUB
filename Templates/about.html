About Us

Agni Dance Club are a local community group and they focus on developing local talent through vocational courses for every age group. We provides outstanding technical training in a positive environment that focuses on the life skills of dependability, punctuality, responsibility, respect, and teamwork. We offer seven(7) different types of dance classes in a variety of styles and levels, so there is a class for everyone! Our classes run every day of the week. Getting involved in Agni Dance Club is a great way for every people to escape from the stresses of life and meet others with a similar passion for dance. We strive to provide everyone with not only the technical dance skills to pursue a professional dance career as should they are interested, but more importantly arm them with essential life skills that lead to success in whatever they pursue.

Our Classes

Tap Dance

Tap dance is a type of dance characterised by using the sounds of tap shoes striking the floor as a form of percussion. The sound is made by shoes that have a metal "tap" on the heel and toe.

Modern Dance

Modern dance is often considered to have emerged as a rejection of, or rebellion against, classical ballet. Socioeconomic and cultural factors also contributed to its development.

Lyrical Dance

Lyrical dance has been defined as a dance style that embodies various aspects of ballet, jazz, acrobatics & Modern dance while still maintaining its autonomy. So You Think You Can Dance.

Jazz Dance

Jazz dance is a performance dance technique and style that first emerged in the United States in the early twentieth century. Jazz dance may refer to vernacular jazz or to Broadway or theatrical jazz. Both genres build on African American vernacular styles of dancing that emerged with jazz music.

HipHop Dance

HipHop is characterized by nine elements, of which only four are considered essential to understanding hip hop musically. The main pillars of hip hop culture, coining the terms: "rapping" (also called MCing or emceeing), a rhythmic vocal rhyming style (orality); DJing (and turntablism), which is making music with record players and DJ mixers (aural/sound and music creation); b-boying/b-girling/breakdancing (movement/dance); and graffiti.

Ballet Dance

Ballet is a type of performance dance that originated during the Italian Renaissance in the fifteenth century and later developed into a concert dance form in France and Russia. It has since become a widespread, highly technical form of dance with its own vocabulary based on French terminology.

Contemporary Dance

Contemporary dance[1] is a genre of dance performance that developed during the mid twentieth century and has since grown to become one of the dominant genres for formally trained dancers throughout the world.
